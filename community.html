Community Guidelines

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Forme is a space for people working through body image, self-confidence, and identity. To keep it safe and supportive for everyone:

Be kind

Treat others the way you'd want to be treated. This includes the language you use, the feedback you give, and how you respond to others' experiences.

No comparison or shaming

Comments that compare bodies, appearances, or progress — whether toward yourself or others — aren't welcome here.

No medical advice

Don't offer diagnoses, recommend medications, or tell others what they should eat or how they should exercise. Share your experience, not prescriptions.

No promotion or spam

Don't advertise products, services, social media accounts, or anything else.

Respect privacy

Don't share personal information about other users.

Violations

Violations may result in removal of content or loss of access to community features.

If you're struggling, please reach out to a professional.
