Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

1. What We Collect

  • Account info: Name, username, and email address you provide during onboarding.
  • Usage data: Messages sent to coaching sessions, check-ins, and app activity.
  • Device info: Anonymous device identifiers for authentication purposes.

2. How We Use It

  • To provide personalized coaching responses.
  • To improve the app experience.
  • To contact you about your account if needed.

3. How We Store It

Your data is stored locally on your device and, where applicable, in secured cloud infrastructure. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Forme uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and data storage.
  • Anthropic — AI coaching responses (messages are processed to generate responses and are not retained for training).
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Apple — in-app purchases.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at info@formeapp.net.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes within the app.
